The idea

As a former dive instructor, I heard The Question so much. Almost every new Open Water student asked: "Where should I go diving next? What is the best diving place in the world?". Well, there is no simple answer to it, as the world is so big and the dive spots are so many. This is why I created this website 'The Question, The Answers' in trying to answer all.

All blogs are solemnly my opinion. Of course not every one will agree. You can only decide if and when you have been there too. But I tried to give a good idea for people that haven't yet had the opportunity to go and so make their decision easier.

If you do have a remark or a question, please contact me.
