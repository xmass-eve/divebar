Taba

Taba is a city on the Sinai Peninsula, at the northern tip of the Gulf of Aqaba, on the border with Israel. Taba Heights lies 20km/12.5mi more to the south. They are newer tourist destinations so dive sites will be more enjoyable and not overcrowded like Sharm El Sheikh.

Taba is a good spot to start your Open Water course. The currents are very gentle in different spots. Visibility is always good in the Red Sea. Water temperatures go from 20C/68F in winter till 30C/86F in summer.

Pharaoh island is a popular small dive island with 5 sites around. It is in a lagoon and completely protected from waves and currents. Another interesting place are the dive sites in The Fjord.
