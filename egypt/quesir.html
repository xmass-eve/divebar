El Quesir

El Quesir is an old town on the southeast coast of mainland Egypt. It is located around 100km/62 miles north of Marsa Alam, so you fly on this city when you come in. Only recently it became a tourist town so it is still very relaxed.

El Quesir has different kind of dive sites, like reefs and wrecks, even some caves for the more experienced diver. The South of Egypt has a bigger chance for seeing the Oceanic White tips, mantas and whale sharks! If you are really lucky, then maybe you can even see a dugong.
Water temperatures go from 20C/68F in winter till 28C/82F in summer. Currents can vary between almost none and strong. The visibility is good, around 25m/82feet.
