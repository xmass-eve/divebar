Australia

The Commonwealth of Australia is the largest island of Oceania. It consists of mainland Australia, Tasmania and some smaller islands. It is the sixth-largest country in the whole world and has the sixth-longest coastline of the world. Australia is surrounded by 35.877km/22.293mi of water, part of it the Indian and the Pacific Ocean, the Timor Sea, Arafura Sea, Coral Sea and Tasman Sea.
